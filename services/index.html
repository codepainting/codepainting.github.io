Design Systems

The idea behind a DS is - in the long run - to make our involvement in the design process as small as possible and allow your team to be as agile as possible. Your team will be able to create whole new project areas with the DS and reserve our input for innovative new concepts or just the most critical areas where our "second look" can help.
